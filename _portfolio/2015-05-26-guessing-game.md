Academy](https://fullstackacademy.com "Fullstack Academy").

Features
--------

-   Cool music once you win or lose.
-   Number validation.
-   Tracking for your previous guesses.
-   Really hard to **NOT** win.
-   Hints
-   Number of guesses limit
-   You can have fun guessing numbers?!
